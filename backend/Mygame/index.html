How to Play

  • Move your mouse (or touch) to control the basket.
  • Catch yellow cheese to earn 10 points.
  • Avoid brown rotten cheese – it costs 20 points!
  • Game lasts 60 seconds. Try to get the highest score!
  • Press Start to begin, Restart to reset.
