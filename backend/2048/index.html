How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or Swipe to move the tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  • After every move, a new tile (2 or 4) will appear in a random empty spot.
  • Get a 2048 tile to win. Keep playing to reach higher scores!
  • Game over when no more moves are possible.
