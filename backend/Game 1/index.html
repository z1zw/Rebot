🧀 Cheese Game

Collect cheese, avoid obstacles, and score points!

Score: 0
Lives: 3

How to Play

  • Use Arrow Keys or WASD to move the mouse.
  • Collect yellow cheese pieces to increase your score.
  • Avoid red obstacles to keep your lives.
  • Each cheese gives +10 points.
  • Each obstacle hit costs 1 life.
  • Game ends when lives reach zero.

On mobile, use the on-screen buttons for movement.

Built with Phaser 3. Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
