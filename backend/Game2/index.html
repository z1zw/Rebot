How to Play 2048

  • Goal: Combine tiles of the same number to reach 2048!
  • Move: Use ↑ ↓ ← → Arrow Keys or Swipe (on touch devices).
  • Merge: When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one with their sum.
  • After each move: A new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty spot.
  • Game Over: When no moves are possible, the game ends.
